Campus Safety Tips

These practical steps can help protect you and your belongings on campus.

Personal Safety

  • Stay alert and aware of your surroundings.
  • Avoid isolated routes, especially at night.
  • Walk in groups whenever possible.

Property Safety

  • Lock your room or office when unattended.
  • Secure valuables and avoid displaying expensive items.
  • Keep important documents in safe places.

Online Safety

  • Protect your passwords and personal information.
  • Be careful with suspicious links and messages.
  • Avoid sharing private details with unknown persons.

Night Safety

  • Use well-lit routes and campus patrol points.
  • Inform someone about your movement plans.
  • Save emergency contact numbers on your phone.

Emergency Preparedness

Know the location of the nearest security office and emergency contact numbers at all times.
