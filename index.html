Welcome to the Security Incident Mapping System

SIM is a web-based platform designed to improve safety awareness and make incident reporting easier for students, staff, landlords, and visitors.

Report an Incident Learn More

About SIM

The platform serves as a communication bridge between the university community and security personnel. It allows users to report incidents, access emergency contacts, learn safety tips, and receive important updates.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

To provide a secure and user-friendly platform that promotes timely reporting and safer university spaces.

Vision

To become a trusted digital solution that strengthens campus security through awareness, collaboration, and technology.

Our Objectives

  • Encourage fast reporting of incidents.
  • Improve communication with security staff.
  • Increase safety awareness across campus.
  • Support quick emergency response.
  • Promote a safe learning environment.

How SIM Works

  1. Open the reporting platform.
  2. Fill out the incident form.
  3. Provide the location and description.
  4. Upload evidence if available.
  5. Submit the report for review.
