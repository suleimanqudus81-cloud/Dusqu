Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about how the system works and how to use it safely.

What is SIM?

SIM is a web-based platform that helps users report campus security incidents and access safety resources.

Who can use SIM?

Students, staff, landlords, visitors, and other members of the university community.

Can I report anonymously?

Yes. Users may choose to submit reports without revealing their identity.

Is my information secure?

Yes. Reports are handled confidentially and shared only with authorized officials.

What incidents can I report?

Theft, robbery, assault, fire outbreak, vandalism, suspicious activity, and other security-related matters.
